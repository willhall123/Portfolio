Hello! My name is Will Hall. I am a local to the Nashville, Tn area however, I am originally from Daytona Beach, Fl. I am a worship musician that volunteers in many local churches. I am also the Worship Director at my home church, The Waters Edge. Since high school, I have worked as a Automotive Technician for Firestone Complete Auto Care and a Technician at CTS Audio Video and Lighting. While maintaining a role as a husband, and working as an Audio Engineer, I have graduated from Vanderbilt Universitys Developer Bootcamp with a Full Stack Developer Certificate. I will be following the same carreer as my father in becoming a Developer. Be sure to follow me on Social Media to keep up with my journey as a Developer!
